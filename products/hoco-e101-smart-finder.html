Description

The Hoco E101 is a smart, compact, and reliable solution to keep track of your valuable items. Designed with dual system support, it works seamlessly with both Apple Find My Network and Google Find Hub Network, making it ideal for users in Bangladesh and worldwide.

Dual Network Compatibility (Apple & Google)

  • Apple Find My Network for iOS users
  • Google Find Hub Network for Android users

This dual compatibility ensures wide device support and accurate location tracking without additional subscription fees.

Compact & Lightweight Design

Made from durable ABS material — Size: 32 × 32 × 7mm, Weight: Only 7.5g. Easily attach it to keys, wallets, bags, luggage, pets, or other important belongings.

Long Battery Life

Powered by a 210mAh / CR2032 button battery with an impressive 6–8 months of battery life, reducing the hassle of frequent battery replacement.

Apple Find My Network Compatibility

  • iPhone SE, iPhone 6s and later (iOS 14.5 or above)
  • iPod touch (7th generation)
  • iPad Pro, iPad (5th generation or later)
  • iPad Air 2 or later, iPad mini 4 or later (iPadOS 14.5 or above)
